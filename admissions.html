Admission Process

At St. Michael's Academy, we seek students who are curious, engaged, and ready to thrive in our rigorous academic environment. We evaluate each applicant holistically, considering academic potential, character, and fit with our school community.

1

Application Submission

Complete the online application or submit a paper form. Include transcripts, teacher recommendations, and application essays.

2

Assessment & Interview

Qualified applicants are invited for an admissions assessment and interview with our admissions team.

3

Decision

Admissions decisions are communicated via email and mail. Accepted students receive enrollment information.

4

Enrollment

Complete enrollment paperwork, submit required documentation, and join the St. Michael's community!

Application Timeline

  • Early Applications: Opens September 1st. Submit applications to be considered for rolling admissions.
  • Regular Deadline: Applications due by January 31st for the following academic year.
  • Application Fee: $75 USD (waivable for families with demonstrated financial need).
  • Decisions: Families are notified of admissions decisions by March 15th.
  • Enrollment Deposit: Due by April 15th to secure your child's place.

Financial Aid & Scholarships

St. Michael's Academy is committed to making our education accessible to talented students from diverse economic backgrounds. We offer need-based financial aid and merit scholarships to qualified applicants. Our Financial Aid Office works with families to create affordable payment plans.
