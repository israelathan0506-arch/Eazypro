Academic Programs

Excellence Across All Disciplines

From language arts to STEM, our comprehensive curriculum prepares students for college and beyond.

Our Curriculum

St. Michael's Academy offers a rigorous, well-balanced curriculum designed to develop critical thinking, creativity, and character. Our courses challenge students to reach their full potential while fostering a love of learning.

Core Academic Departments

  • English Language Arts: Comprehensive instruction in reading, writing, speaking, and critical analysis. Students engage with literature, rhetoric, and composition.
  • Mathematics: From foundational numeracy to advanced calculus and statistics. Our math program builds problem-solving skills and mathematical reasoning.
  • Science: Biology, chemistry, physics, and environmental science. Hands-on labs, field studies, and research opportunities enhance learning.
  • Social Studies: History, geography, civics, and world cultures. We explore human societies and develop global awareness.
  • World Languages: Spanish, French, Mandarin, and Latin. Language instruction includes cultural immersion and practical communication skills.
  • Arts: Visual arts, music, drama, and design. Creative expression is cultivated across multiple disciplines and grade levels.
  • Physical Education & Athletics: Comprehensive PE program plus competitive and recreational sports options for all skill levels.

Advanced Learning Opportunities

Advanced Placement

Students in Upper School can enroll in AP courses in subjects ranging from Biology to World History. AP classes prepare students for college-level work and nationally recognized examinations.

Honors Courses

Accelerated courses for high-achieving students beginning in Middle School. Honors classes offer greater depth and complexity in core subjects.

Independent Study

Advanced students have the opportunity to pursue independent research projects with faculty mentorship, exploring topics of personal interest at a deep level.

Electives & Seminars

Wide variety of electives including robotics, digital media, entrepreneurship, creative writing, philosophy, and more.

Learning Support

We believe in supporting every learner. Our Learning Specialists provide individualized support for students with different learning styles. We offer tutoring, accommodations, and adaptive strategies to ensure all students can access the curriculum and succeed.
